Mycological Spectrum (MS)


The Association of Fungal Biologists (AFB) publishes its official open-access, peer-reviewed journal, Mycological Spectrum. This international journal serves as a vital platform for fungal research and innovation, promoting high-quality scientific contributions across diverse fields of mycology. Visit Journal


Message from the Editorial Board


Mycological Spectrum is a progressive and inclusive scientific journal dedicated to advancing fungal biology, biotechnology, taxonomy, biochemistry, genetics, evolution, ecology, and conservation. It welcomes contributions from researchers worldwide, fostering interdisciplinary collaboration and innovation in mycology.

Aims and Scope


Mycological Spectrum provides a high-quality research platform covering fundamental and applied aspects of fungal sciences. We publish:

  • - Systematics, Taxonomy, and Phylogeny
  • -Fungal Ecology and Conservation
  • - Clinical and Medical Mycology
  • - Industrial Applications of Fungi
  • - Genetics and Evolution
  • - Fungal Biotechnology and Bioinformatics

Publication Ethics Statement


The journal enforces a double-blind peer review process and follows strict ethical standards. As part of our commitment to research integrity, we are working towards membership with COPE (Centre for Publication Ethics) to ensure the highest ethical publishing standards.
