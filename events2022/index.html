Events from 2022 - 2025


Since its inception in April 2022, the Association of Fungal Biologists (AFB) has been actively engaged in fostering mycological research and education through a diverse range of events, webinars, conferences, and competitions. AFB has organized national and international webinars featuring renowned experts, covering topics such as fungal biotechnology, antifungal resistance, myco-drug development, lichen biodiversity, and scientific writing. The association has collaborated with esteemed institutions like SIES College, Savitribai Phule Pune University (Pune, MycoAsia, MycoIndia, NFCCI-ARI, and JSS Academy of Higher Education & Research to conduct workshops on phylogenetics, publication ethics, and fungal morphology. Notable events include the International Conference on Biology, Biodiversity, and Biotechnology of Fungi (2023) and the International Conference on the Role of Fungi in Sustainable Development (2024). Additionally, AFB has celebrated World Fungus Day annually with expert talks and competitions, such as the Fungal Photography Contest. The association continues to provide a platform for mycologists, researchers, and students to engage in knowledge exchange, innovation, and community building in fungal sciences.
