Professor Kamal Merit Award for Young Researchers

Periodicity of the Award

The award shall be conferred annually to the winners of the Oral Presentation (03 Nos).

Eligibility Criteria

  • Active young faculty/professionals not exceeding 45 years of age.
  • Excellent contributions to fungal biodiversity, taxonomy, systematics, and applications.
  • Only for permanent faculty/professionals.
  • Ph.D. scholars are not eligible.

Administration of the Award

The Association for Fungal Biodiversity (AFB) shall have the sole right of selection of candidates and the formulation or revision of rules governing the selection process.

Value of the Award

The winners will receive a memento and a citation. The award does not carry any cash prize.

Selection Process

  • AFB will invite nominations from young, active faculty/professionals.
  • A separate award competition will be held during the annual conference.
  • A three-member judging committee will select winners based on Oral Presentations.
  • The decision of the committee is final. Canvassing will lead to disqualification.

Presentation of the Award

The award will be presented during the valedictory session of the annual conference.
