INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE


On Role of Fungi in Sustainable Development - From Exploration to Application

2nd Annual Meet of Association of Fungal Biologists (AFB)

Organized by

Department of Botany, Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune 411007, India

Prof. Ramkrishna More Arts, Commerce and Science College, Akurdi, Pune 411044, India

Date & Venue

Date: October 23-25, 2024

Venue: Department of Botany, Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune 411007, India

Themes Covered

  • Mycodiversity, Taxonomy, Systematics and Myco-tourism
  • Prospects of Fungal Conservation and Regulatory Framework
  • Clinical and Medical Mycology
  • Host-parasite Interaction and Biology of Fungal Endophytes
  • Fungal Molecules and Enzymes for Diverse Applications
  • Prospects of Cultivation of Edible and Medicinal Mushrooms
  • Fungal Biomass, Bioenergy and Sustainable Uses
  • Bioinformatics and Molecular Approaches in Mycology
  • Interdisciplinary Perspectives of Fungal Biology
  • Prospects of Fungal 'Omics
  • Fungi in Agriculture
  • Fungi in Industry
  • Fungi in Nanotechnology
  • Conveners & Organizing Secretary
